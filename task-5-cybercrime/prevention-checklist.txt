Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

2. Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.

3. Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID and name before sending money.

4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media.

5. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

6. Download apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

7. Regularly check your bank account and transaction history for any unauthorized activity.

8. Do not trust calls or messages that create urgency, such as “your account will be blocked.”

9. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

10. In case of any cyber fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

11. You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930 for quick assistance.
