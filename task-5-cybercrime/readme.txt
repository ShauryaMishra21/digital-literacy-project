While researching UPI fraud, I was surprised by how easily people can be tricked into losing money within seconds. The most shocking part was that many victims unknowingly approve payment requests, thinking they are receiving money instead of sending it.

This task helped me understand the importance of being cautious while using digital payment platforms. I realized that awareness is the first step in preventing cybercrime. One habit I will personally change is to carefully verify every transaction request before approving it and never share sensitive information like OTPs or PINs with anyone.

Overall, this task made me more aware and responsible as a digital user.
