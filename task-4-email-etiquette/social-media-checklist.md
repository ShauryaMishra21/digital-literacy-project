***Social Media Do’s \& Don’ts:***



**Do’s:**



* Use strong passwords and enable 2FA
* Keep your profiles professional
* Think before posting anything
* Verify information before sharing
* Respect others online



**Don’ts:**



* Don’t share OTPs or personal details
* Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
* Don’t click on unknown links
* Don’t engage in online arguments
* Don’t accept requests from strangers
